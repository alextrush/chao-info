Boundless cuteness to create however you can imagine

Chao are customizable NPCs in the Sonic Adventure and Advance series which you can raise in virtual gardens

Alignment

Good VS Evil

Alignment decides whether your chao grows into a Hero or a Dark chao

Petting, feeding or harming your chao will change their alignment

Hero chao get a halo and brighter colours

Dark chao get a spiky ball and darker colours

Middle values of alignment will leave chao unchanged

Skills

Grow Your Chao

In addition to the alignment value there are 5 other values which visibly affect your chao

Swim, Fly, Run, Power and Stamina

Stamina can be raised by feeding your chao various fruits in the gardens

The remaining stats can be changed by giving your chao animals and chaos drives

There are two hidden stats for Intelligence and Luck, affected only by chaos drives

Events

Play Together

There are various minigames that you can play to test the strength of your chao

Yes, chao is in fact the plural of chao, chaos is a separate concept

In chao racing you race 7 other chao across several varied race tracks

In chao karate, you face off in a 5 match tournament to see whose the strongest chao warrior

In Sonic Adventure 2, chao minigames lock 5% of the emblems found in the game

Make Your Chao Go Wild

Animals found in Sonic levels can be brought into the chao gardens

By playing with those animals, chao obtain various abilities and cosmetic changes
